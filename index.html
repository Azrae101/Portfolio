About Me

Hi, I'm Clara Holst

I'm a Cognitive Science student at Aarhus University passionate about turning research on learning and memory into practical, meaningful tools for education. My work sits at the intersection of data science, AI, and human-centered design, where I build interactive systems that make learning more engaging, effective, and grounded in how the brain actually works.

This focus comes to life through projects ranging from CogCards: a gamified learning platform based on cognitive principles; to educational simulations, data-driven visualizations, and interactive games. Whether I’m modeling misinformation spread with agent-based systems, designing critical data exhibitions, or building puzzle-based learning experiences, I’m driven by the question: How can we make complex knowledge more intuitive, playful, and impactful?
